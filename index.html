CTOaaS

"CTO as a service" is not a new concept for the software industry. There are many companies that leverage and benefit from CTOaaS. The primary advantage is that you only pay for what you use. Some possible scenarios include:

  • you have a game changing idea but do not know how to realize it technologically
  • you hire a remote team but need someone to validate their work
  • your company does not require a full-time CTO at its current stage
  • you need external objective technical advice
  • filling a temporary gap in technical leadership

DragonTech CTOaaS can assist in:

  • strategy and vision
  • building and leading the technology team
  • software architecture, tools, and choosing technology stack
  • defining and implement process and software development life cycle
  • working with product leadership to ensure that solutions will scale to the company goal

Product Engineering

Developing software is not just about software. It is all about delivering value to the customer. Product engineering as opposed to software engineering involves positioning the developer closer to the customer. Having the developer interact directly with the customer will ensure that nothing is "lost in translation". Gathering both functional and non-functional requirements that are clearly understood by both parties will ultimately result in a superior product.
